Open data manifesto

Why open?

The travel industry runs on closed APIs and rented data. We think you deserve better — a stack you can audit, fork, host yourself, and trust. OpenTrails is built on that principle.

  • Every data point is sourced from a public, citable API.
  • Every map tile comes from OpenStreetMap, under the ODbL.
  • Every itinerary discloses its inputs, costs, and reasoning.
  • Every page is keyboard accessible and respects reduced-motion preferences.
  • Code is MIT-licensed and reproducible.

Privacy

What we don't do.

We don't track you across the web. We don't load third-party ads or analytics. We don't sell, share, or rent your data. The only requests OpenTrails makes are the open APIs the page tells you about — and we link to every one.
